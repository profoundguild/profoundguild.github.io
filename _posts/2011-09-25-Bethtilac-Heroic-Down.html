We’ve had some trouble with Majordomo and Rhyolith recently, for various different reasons, so we decided to try for a more technical fight to take a break from the brute force of damage and healing on the other heroic modes.

After attempting the boss around 21 times, we finally got the kill 4 hours into the raid, at overtime, and what a deserving kill this was.

Everything seemed to just click into place at the start of the fight, with no fatal mistakes and some quick footing on the Broodling poppers.
