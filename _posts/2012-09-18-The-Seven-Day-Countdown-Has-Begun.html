Twitter and Facebook are beginning to show signs of excitement for Blizzard’s 4th expansion to World of Warcraft, Mists of Pandaria, and it’s time to stir up some excitement we’re all feeling within the guild too.

Last night Blizzard opened the Theramore’s Fall Scenario, in which players experience the island destroyed through the conflict of the Alliance and Horde. This marks the beginning of the 7 day countdown, after which this scenario closes and will reactivate again at level 90.
