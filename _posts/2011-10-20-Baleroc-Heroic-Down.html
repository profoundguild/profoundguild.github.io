We took down Baleroc heroic tonight with some very frustrating 3-4% wipes, hitting the enrage purely because of deaths bringing the overall DPS down.

A very intensive fight, given that at any time something can go hugely wrong. 2 out of 3 healers with the debuff? Check. Someone died on a shard? Check. We have all the luck.
