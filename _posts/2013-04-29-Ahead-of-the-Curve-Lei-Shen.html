We defeated Lei Shen on Sunday with a chunk of help from alts and ex. raiders in Dubious who filled our free spots.

We’re still filling our raid team up, so we’re not quite ready for our regular schedule of Wednesdays and Sundays, but when we are I’m sure you’ll see a few posts here and there with our success stories and other things.

Thanks again to Dubious for the help and support they’ve given us!
