UPDATE: Wowhead 4.0.6 Patch Notes

After all the hotfixing and hype about class changes, finally all those changes we heard about on the PTR will be released to retail (updated!) this week (7th/9th February US/EU).

There’s a very good summary of changes over on MMO-Champion.com with full retail patch notes styled and formatted for your reading pleasure.

Wowhead have also done the round-up of the PTR patch notes which are as detailed as always (although I think MMO-Champion’s are more accurate at this time).

Some of the important changes we should all be aware of are:

  • Overall class changes
  • Much anticipated dungeon nerfs/buffs
  • The Armadillo Pup is now available to revered guild members
  • Dungeon finder group difficulty now stacks at 5% per random member
  • Guild group XP can now be attained at 60% members for 50% XP, 80% for 100% XP and 100% for 150% XP (got that?)
  • Tol Barad is now easier to attack, with a 200% capture speed bonus when 2 keeps are controlled
  • All crafted epics now get a new gem socket

I would recommend reading all the changes made to your class. There are some very important ones, even ones that may rethink your role in raids. Also, check out our own comments on the patch notes in the forums.

Good luck!
