We’ve been banging our head against the wall on this guy for two weeks. Multiple 6% wipes and lots of stress with disconnects and AFKs had us at his mercy, and almost had me begging the boss for a swift death.

But, at last, tonight we managed to kill him. We took 2 people outside the guild, which feels like we didn’t deserve it much, but we got the achievement so who’s counting?
