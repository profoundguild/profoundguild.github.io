It’s that time of year again. The sun is out, the holidays are here, and the Winter burn-out has hit us hard.

All the usual Spring issues are upon us, but we are not yet done with this tier of raid content. With only Nefarian and Al’Akir left to pummel, we are at an ideal position where any new recruits will get to experience first-time kills of end-raid bosses, learning the encounter from start to finish, and reap the glory of being one of the two arguably most difficult roles while doing so.

We are looking for both tanks (Warrior, DK or Bear) and healers (Shaman or Paladin) and encourage all active, punctual, fun and sometimes damn crazy people to apply now!

Applications over the weekend will be fast-tracked for invites before Monday’s raid. If you are accepted as a raider, you will be raiding (we do not recruit for “bench”, only priority or casual raid roles).

Raid times are:

  • Monday: 20:00 to 23:00 server time
  • Wednesday: 20:00 to 23:00 server time
  • Thursday: 20:00 to 23:00 server time

Good luck!
