We had a windfall of success on Wednesday where we stepped into Heart of Fear and found things were simply working as intended from the start. Imperial Vizier Zor’lok is one of those bosses where you really want to know why people are dying but you’re so spread out or dealing with your own crap to help them. Attenuation has always been a fun mechanic, but put the thought of doing anything but surviving to the back of your mind and you can breeze through it so easily – I think this is where we finally got it right (except the huge boom of deaths right at the end).

After a tense Blade Lord Ta’yak kill (heightened by nerves from Zor’lok) we scooted over to Garalon and managed to take it down in the third pull of the night. Normally I would say that’s impressive, but he’s so easy it feels shameful to do it more than the first pull – perfectionists are never happy.

{% include frameimage.html index=530 caption="Legless" alt="Heroic Garalon Kill Shot" src="http://www.profoundguild.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/WoWScrnShot_121212_225350-300x168.jpg" url="/uploads/2012/12/WoWScrnShot_121212_225350.jpg" previewurl="/uploads/2012/12/WoWScrnShot_121212_225350-300x168.jpg" %}

Overall a good day of raiding, and there’s still 2 raid days left in the week for us to make some headway on more bosses!
