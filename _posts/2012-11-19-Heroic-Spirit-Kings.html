We finally got a stroke of luck on pulls last night on Reset Kings giving us 6 full tries out of 9 pulls, allowing us to take Subetai 2nd, Zian 3rd and Meng last to net the kill right on the berserk. We definitely need to improve on some things, but if we manage to get a kill video up you’ll see how easily it can get messy.

Imagine Flanking Orders leading into a Pillage which is instantaneously followed by a Maddening Shout. We had that.
