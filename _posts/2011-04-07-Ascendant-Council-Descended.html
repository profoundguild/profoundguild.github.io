Since this was quite a technical fight for everyone involved, we weren’t sure we would be able to get it down in the one night. But, we spent only 2 hours and wiped 9 times, two of which were on 400k and 20k (less than 1%).

This was another exhilarating kill, and had me shaking for a few moments afterwards. I was so busy kiting the boss, I didn’t even see him go down at first!
