It’s always disheartening hitting a wall like this for a progression raiding guild. In fact, I would hazard a guess to say that half of the weeks since the Blackhorn kill, we’ve actually just been victim to real life issues. It has been hard to get the group set up, but tonight we had it perfect.

We didn’t even change tactics, and there’s the kill. That 5% nerf probably wasn’t needed – actually, I’m just gonna go out and say it: we didn’t need the nerf. It’s a shame, but say what you will.
