--- index: 489 title: "Heroic Gara’jal the Spiritbinder is Down!" author: Sleuth date: 2012-10-21 02:30:29 categories: [Guild, News] tags: [gara'jal the spiritbinder, heroic, kill shot, mogu'shan vaults, progression] ---

We pooled every last bit of effort tonight to push the hardest we can at getting Gara’jal the Spiritbinder down; he’s a real challenge and seems to have a lot of RNG based problems. We gave ourselves a 20 second lead by swapping to the higher enchants, which I recommend every guild do to save yourselves some time! It was a very long process but we have it done, and we can now settle back down into learning the next fight.

This boss shall henceforth be known as “The Twat”.
