It’s just like everybody says.

You get through Spine, and think to yourself “nothing can be more difficult than that, surely?” and you’d be right. We spent about a third of the time on Madness compared to Spine, and now it all seemed so easy.

{% include frameimage.html index=390 caption="Taste the Rainbow" alt="Heroic Madness of Deathwing Kill Shot" url="/uploads/2012/05/WoWScrnShot_051412_214102.jpg" previewurl="/uploads/2012/05/WoWScrnShot_051412_214102-300x168.jpg" %}

The biggest problem with this encounter is the lack of working immunity abilities for Impale. Are we always going to be limited to using a DK? I’d like my Ardent Defender to do what it says in the tooltip, and Guardian Spirit was pretty annoying too. But after all the planning, the hours of wipes, and the log browsing, we finally settled on a group and went ahead.

A very well-deserved kill from the entire guild – we would not be here without all members past and present! This is for you; all of you.
